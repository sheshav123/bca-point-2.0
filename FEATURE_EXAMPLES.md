## ğŸ¯ Example 7: Seasonal Content

### Scenario
Adjust ad-free status based on academic calendar.

### During Regular Semester
- 10% materials ad-free (basic references)
- 90% materials with ads

### During Exam Period (2 weeks before exams)
- 30% materials ad-free (important references, formulas)
- 70% materials with ads

### During Holidays
- 15% materials ad-free (light reading, announcements)
- 85% materials with ads

### Implementation
Use the toggle switch in admin panel to quickly change ad-free status:
1. Before exams: Toggle important materials to ad-free
2. After exams: Toggle back to with-ads
3. Takes 2 minutes to update 20-30 materials

### Result
- Flexible strategy based on student needs
- Higher satisfaction during stressful periods
- Maintained revenue during regular periods

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### 1. Start Small
- Make 5 materials ad-free
- Add images to 5 materials
- Monitor for 1 week
- Adjust based on data

### 2. Listen to Students
- Check which materials are accessed most
- Read feedback and reviews
- Adjust ad-free status accordingly

### 3. Seasonal Adjustments
- More ad-free during exams
- More images for visual subjects
- Regular content during normal periods

### 4. Quality Over Quantity
- Better to have 5 great ad-free materials than 50 mediocre ones
- Better to have 10 high-quality images than 100 low-quality ones

### 5. Clear Communication
- Label ad-free materials clearly
- Mention image count in descriptions
- Set student expectations
